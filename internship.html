OJT ENROLLMENT

NOVEMBER 28, 2025

The journey began with my OJT after I officially enrolled on November 28, 2025. This marked the beginning of an exciting chapter where I would transition from academic learning to practical, real-world experience. The enrollment process was straightforward, requiring me to submit necessary documents and complete all the required forms. I felt a mix of excitement and nervousness as I embarked on this new adventure, knowing that it would be a significant step toward my professional career.

PRE-INTERNSHIP PREP

DECEMBER 05, 2025

Before starting the actual internship, I participated in a workshop for internship readiness. The workshop covered various essential topics to prepare me for the internship experience. It taught me to be more professional and responsible in the workplace. The assessment involved acting out scenarios that typically happen in the workplace and how to handle them properly. It was a fun and insightful experience that helped build my confidence and prepared me for real-world challenges.

RESUME PREPARATION

PREPARATION PHASE

Resume preparation was a crucial step in my internship journey. I dedicated time to crafting a professional resume that highlighted my skills, experiences, and academic achievements. This document served as my first impression to potential employers and was essential for securing internship opportunities. I carefully selected relevant projects, coursework, and extracurricular activities that demonstrated my capabilities and passion for my field.
