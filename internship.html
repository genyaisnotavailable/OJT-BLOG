OJT ENROLLMENT

NOVEMBER 28, 2025

The journey began with my OJT after I officially enrolled on November 28, 2025. This marked the beginning of an exciting chapter where I would transition from academic learning to practical, real-world experience.

PRE-INTERNSHIP PREP

PREPARATION PHASE

Before starting the actual internship, I participated in a workshop for internship readiness. The workshop covered various essential topics to prepare me for the internship experience. It taught me to be more professional and responsible in the workplace. The assessment involved acting out scenarios that typically happen in the workplace and how to handle them properly. It was a fun and insightful experience.

RESUME

PREPARATION PHASE

Resume preparation was a crucial step in my internship journey. I dedicated time to crafting a professional resume that highlighted my skills, experiences, and academic achievements. This document served as my first impression to potential employers and was essential for securing internship opportunities.
